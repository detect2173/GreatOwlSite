Automation Glossary

Updated: September 10, 2025

Key Terms

  • Automation: Using software to perform tasks without manual effort.
  • Flow: A sequence of steps your chatbot follows to guide a user.
  • Trigger: An event (like a button click or keyword) that starts an automation.
  • Integration: Connecting your chatbot to other tools like Google Sheets or a CRM.
  • ManyChat: A platform we use to build and host chatbots.

How To Use This Glossary

Use this page as a quick reference while planning your chatbot project. If you come across a term you don’t recognize, reach out and we’ll clarify it in the context of your business.
