How To Automate Google Reviews

Updated: September 10, 2025

Overview

Automating review requests helps you collect more 5-star reviews without manual chasing. Our chatbots guide customers at the right time with the right message and a direct link to your Google profile.

Best Practices

  • Ask shortly after a positive interaction.
  • Make it one tap: include your Google review link.
  • Use gentle reminders (e.g., 24 hours and 7 days later).
  • Route unhappy customers to a private feedback form.
  • Track results to optimize timing and messaging.
